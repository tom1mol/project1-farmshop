About us

We are a family run business that started out in 2003. We are located just outside the town of Athboy in the heart of County Meath. The farm is run by the son of the house Jeremy and his wife Denise. The shop is run by our lovely daughter Helga and her husband Olef. My wife Jean and I (Dominic) run the online side of the business and deal directly with You, our online customers.
